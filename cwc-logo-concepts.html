Notes on use

Concept 01 (The Still Point) is the strongest for the CWC philosophy — chaos converging to clarity is the entire thesis of the work. Works beautifully as a favicon, profile photo, and site header mark.

Concept 02 (The Spiral) is more meditative — better for book covers, retreat materials, or anything about the inner journey specifically.

Concept 03 is the most versatile — the mark + wordmark combination works across all contexts. C·W·C as an embossed stamp has real presence.
