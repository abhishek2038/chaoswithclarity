CWC — The Breath Mark

Two spirals, one breath.
Chaos in. Clarity out.

The mark is two spirals interleaved — offset by half a turn, converging to the same still point. One is the inhale: the world rushing in, the noise, the forest of thought. The other is the exhale: the settling, the return, the I am that. They are not opposites. They are one continuous movement.
